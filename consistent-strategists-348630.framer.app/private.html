DATA SECURITY

We protect your data by adhering to industry standard regulations on data protection, categorizing it into data types (e.g. technical data, personal data, cookie data, etc.) while applying relevant safeguards and due diligence procedures. Therefore, we apply appropriate technical and organizational controls to protect your data, including secure computer systems that adopt least privilege access to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification or destruction of personal data. All printed documents are locked in a secure cabinet and destroyed when no longer required.

Encryption of your data in transit. Encryption provides a high level of security and privacy for your personal data. When personal data is provided when using our Services, we use strong encryption technologies (such as Transport Layer Security) to protect personal data during transmission from your devices to our servers.

To provide additional trust and security, we use digital EV (Extended Validation) Certificates issued by trusted Certificate Authorities. You can see the 'Green Bar' in supported browser versions, which confirms that all personal data transmitted is secure.

Protecting your personal data in our infrastructure. We prioritize developing Services that are secure by default. By default, our Services are designed with strict security requirements in mind before we even begin development. This is key to ensuring the protection and privacy of all personal data that our Services handle and store once the service or new feature is released.

To protect personal data, we use pseudonymization, which allows most of our Services to operate without using your real data. Instead, our Services use an untraceable system ID to identify you.

Codersmaster is always vigilant about the security of personal data stored in our infrastructure. That is why we locate all of our equipment used to process your personal data in secure, proprietary data centers. Network access to this equipment is isolated from the Internet. We use network segmentation to isolate Services that require different levels of security from each other. Furthermore, we restrict logical access to personal data to our employees on a “need to know” basis. Therefore, only those personnel who truly need access to personal data for the purpose of providing you with our best Service will have access to it.

Threat Protection. Codersmaster is highly informed about modern threats to personal data security and privacy, and we are well prepared to combat them. All events occurring in our infrastructure are continuously monitored, analyzed and responded to, which allows us to ensure adequate protection of your personal data, keeping it safe from threats, vulnerabilities and the effects of malware.

In the event of a failure that affects the accessibility of your personal data, we have personal data backup and recovery procedures in place that will help us restore your personal data in a short time. To further ensure fast recovery, we have high availability mode enabled for most of the critical databases, which allows us to minimize downtime
